Frequently Asked Questions

Can you help with product research and differentiation for Amazon only?

While we specialize in Amazon product research, our methodologies can be applied to other e-commerce platforms as well. We focus on Amazon because of its unique data availability and established marketplace dynamics.

What is your product development process?

Our 4-phase process includes: 1) Market & Opportunity Analysis, 2) Product Validation & Differentiation, 3) Product Development & Iteration, and 4) Brand Positioning & Listing Optimization. Each phase is backed by data and customer insights.

What do you prioritize in the product selection/validation process?

We prioritize: 1) Market demand (search volume and trends), 2) Competition level and quality, 3) Profit potential after all costs, 4) Product differentiation opportunities, and 5) Long-term brand building potential.

How do you present the DATA?

We provide comprehensive reports including: 1) Market analysis summary, 2) Competitive landscape, 3) Customer demand metrics, 4) Opportunity scorecards, and 5) Actionable recommendations. All data is visualized for easy understanding.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes, we're happy to sign NDAs to protect your product ideas and business information. We take client confidentiality very seriously.
